# MealMate AI

## 1. Introduction

MealMate AI is an AI-powered Smart Meal Planning Platform designed to simplify daily meal planning for families, students, working professionals, hostels, and offices.

The platform generates personalized daily, weekly, and monthly meal plans based on user preferences, dietary habits, health goals, budget, location, season, and lifestyle.

Instead of manually deciding "What should we cook today?", users receive an intelligent meal schedule along with recipes, nutritional information, grocery lists, and cooking videos.

The application aims to reduce decision fatigue, improve nutrition, save time, minimize food waste, and make meal planning easier through Artificial Intelligence.

## 2. Problem Statement

Every day, millions of people face the same question:

"What should we cook today?"

Meal planning is usually done manually and often ignores factors such as nutrition, season, budget, health conditions, dietary preferences, and available ingredients.

Families, students, and working professionals frequently spend unnecessary time deciding meals, resulting in repetitive food choices, unhealthy eating habits, increased food waste, and poor grocery planning.

MealMate AI solves this problem by providing intelligent, personalized, and customizable meal plans generated according to each user's lifestyle and preferences.

## 3. Objectives

The primary objectives of MealMate AI are:

- Provide personalized meal recommendations.
- Generate daily, weekly, and monthly meal plans.
- Support different dietary preferences and health goals.
- Recommend recipes with cooking instructions.
- Suggest grocery lists automatically.
- Allow users to customize meal schedules.
- Support family, hostel, office, and group meal planning.
- Reduce food waste and improve nutrition.
- Integrate Artificial Intelligence for smarter meal recommendations.
